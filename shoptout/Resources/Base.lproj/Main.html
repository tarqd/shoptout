Enable shoptout Safari Extension on iOS

Method 1: From Settings

  1. Open the Settings app on your iOS device
  2. Scroll down and tap on Safari
  3. Tap on Extensions
  4. Find shoptout in the list and tap on it
  5. Toggle the switch to enable the extension

Method 2: From Safari (Recommended)

  1. Open Safari on your iOS device
  2. Navigate to any website
  3. Tap the Share icon (box with an arrow) in the toolbar
  4. Scroll horizontally and tap Extensions
  5. Select shoptout from the list
  6. Toggle the switch to enable the extension
  7. When prompted, select "Always Allow on Every Website" for best experience

Once enabled, you can use shoptout while browsing in Safari!
